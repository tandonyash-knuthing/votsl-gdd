Part One

The Journey to Betrayal

"He fights alone, haunted by the memory of failure."

Narrative Context

The Prelude: After a grueling 20-year crusade, the Hero's party finally vanquished the Demon King. They returned as legends, celebrating a month of hard-won peace in the village.

The Betrayal: However, shadows lingered where light faded. In a brutal surprise attack, the King's army and the Church razed the village. The Dwarf was slaughtered. The Elf was kidnapped. The Hero and Priest were left broken and half-dead.

The Depression (6 Months): Believing his entire party slain, the Hero fell into a deep, paralyzing depression. For six months, he mourned, his skills atrophying. He is only roused when the scarred Priest finds him with a revelation: "She is alive."

Climax (H3): The Hero sets out, regaining his strength. He confronts a shadowy figure from his past, only to uncover a truth that shatters his resolve. In the ruins of the fight, he finds a Child imprisoned.

Hero's Core Mechanics

Block & Parry

Hold to Block (reduces damage). Tap just before impact (0.15s window) to Parry, negating damage and restoring 5 MP.

Dodge

Standard evasion roll with i-frames. Essential for positioning.

Perfect Dodge (Teleport)

Unlocked later. A frame-perfect dodge (0.075s window) that instantly teleports the Hero a short distance. Cost: 0 MP.

The Umbra Pact (Rage Mode)

Unlocked at H3 Climax. A risk/reward toggle representing the Hero's grief.

Part Two

The Stewardship

"He becomes the Shield, so she can become the Storm."

The Trust System

Combat becomes asymmetric. The Hero must defend to enable the Child's DPS.

Duo Synergy

  • Hero: Tanks & Parries (0.15s window).
  • Child: Auto-Casts Magic if Hero is safe.
  • Panic: Hero Hit = 5s Silence (0 DPS).

Elemental Imbue (New)

Unlocked at H8. Hero spends 50 MP to coat his sword with the Child's current element (Fire/Ice/Poison) for 30s.

Part Three

The Descent & The Choice

"When the light fades, will you become the darkness to save it?"

Path A: The Path of Sacrifice

Reject the Blade (Hard Mode)

  • Narrative: The Hero accepts the painful truth. He refuses the delusion of the blade.
  • Gameplay: SOLO Combat. No Magic. Pure Skill. The ultimate test of the Hero's base mechanics.
  • Goal: Rescue the Child to regain magic for the final confrontation.

Path B: The Path of Vengeance

Accept the Blade (Power Fantasy)

  • Narrative: The Hero believes the lie within the sword. Madness consumes him.
  • Gameplay: Soul-Forged Blade. Rapid-fire Corruption. No cooldowns. Unstoppable power.
  • Goal: Slaughter the King at any cost.
