The Vigil of the Second Light

"Duty vs. Healing: A 2D Action-Platformer about protecting the light while fighting the dark within."

Trust Mechanic

Asymmetric combat where the Hero protects the Child to maintain DPS. Failure causes Silence.

Hope-Gating

Progression is tied to emotional healing. Defeating trauma (bosses) unlocks abilities.

Precision Defense

High-stakes 0.15s parry windows reward rhythm and discipline over button mashing.

Narrative & Premise

The story begins six months after a catastrophic attack. The Hero, broken by the loss of their partner (The Elf), has fallen into a state of "Depression," losing their advanced combat abilities. They are now tasked with protecting a mysterious Child, whose survival is key to the kingdom's future.

The Hero

A stoic protector. Mechanical "nerfs" at the start represent grief. Focuses on Defense/Melee.

The Child

A young magic user representing the "Second Light." High damage but requires safety to cast.

Core Mechanics

The Trust System (Combat)

The core loop revolves around protecting the Child to maintain DPS. If the Hero misses a parry, the Child enters a Panic State.

  • Hero: Tank / Parry / Positioning
  • Child: Glass Cannon / Elemental Magic
  • Failure: 5-second Silence (0 DPS)

The Umbra Pact (Risk/Reward)

Unlocked at Act I Climax. A toggle ability that trades safety for extreme power.

The Sacrifice

Instantly reduces HP to 50% (Hard cap at 100 HP).

The Reward

+10 MP/sec Regen AND 2x Melee Damage for 30s.

Progression: "Hope-Gating"

Progression is strictly tied to the narrative. The Hero begins in a state of depression (mechanically limited) and regains abilities by overcoming trauma (Bosses).

Hub Boss / Location Ability Unlocked Narrative Flashback
H1 The Royal Scavenger Double Jump "We all carry a piece of the distance."
H2 Glimmering Abyss Glide "Do not mistake a path for a barrier."
H3 The Priest (Act I Boss) Umbra Pact The Betrayal revealed. Hero enters Rage Mode.
H5 Lord Vesper Poison Magic (Child) "Control over dark expression is pragmatic."

Technical Implementation (Unity)

Input System

Utilizing Unity's new Input System to ensure frame-perfect detection for the 0.15s parry window.

Child AI (FSM)

Implementing a Finite State Machine: Idle, Casting, Follow, and Panic (triggered by PlayerHit event).
